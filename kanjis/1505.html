Kanji Details:

澄
  • Frequency:1722
  • Kun: す.む, す.ます, -す.ます
  • On: チョウ
  • Meaning: lucidity, be clear, clear, clarify, settle, strain, look grave
  • Examples:
    WordReadingMeaning
    清澄せいちょうclear, serene
    澄むすむto clear (e.g. weather), to become transparent
    澄ますすますto clear, to make clear, to be unruffled, to look unconcerned, to look demure, to look prim, to put on airs
    澄み切るすみきるto be serene
    研ぎ澄ますとぎすますto sharpen, to grind, to whet, to hone, to make keen
    澄み切ったすみきったperfectly clear
    澄まし顔すましがおcomposed expression, clear face
    お澄ましおすましprim and proper girl
    見澄ますみすますto observe carefully, to make sure
    耳を澄ますみみをすますto listen carefully, to strain one's ears
