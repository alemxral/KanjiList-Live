代
  • Frequency:66
  • Kun: か.わる, かわ.る, かわ.り, -がわ.り, か.える, よ, しろ
  • On: ダイ, タイ
  • Meaning: substitute, change, convert, replace, period, age, counter for decades of ages, eras, etc., generation, charge, rate, fee
