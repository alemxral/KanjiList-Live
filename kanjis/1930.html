漬
  • Frequency:1818
  • Kun: つ.ける, つ.かる, -づ.け, -づけ
  • On: シ
  • Meaning: pickling, soak, moisten, steep
  • Examples:

    茶漬けちゃづけrice with ocha poured on (Japanese dish)

    漬るひたるto be soaked in, to be flooded, to be immersed in
    どっぷりと漬かるどっぷりとつかるto be deeply submerged
