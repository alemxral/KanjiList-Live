悩
  • Frequency:1084
  • Kun: なや.む, なや.ます, なや.ましい, なやみ
  • On: ノウ
  • Meaning: trouble, worry, in pain, distress, illness
  • Examples:
    WordReadingMeaning
    悩みなやみtrouble(s), worry, distress, sorrows, anguish, agony, problem
    悩ますなやますto afflict, to torment, to harass, to molest
    苦悩くのうsuffering, distress, affliction, anguish, agony, trouble
    悩むなやむto be worried, to be troubled
    伸び悩むのびなやむto be sluggish (business)
    悩ましいなやましいseductive, carnal, enchanting; troubling, difficult, thorny, hard; anxious, uneasy
    思い悩むおもいなやむto worry
    煩悩ぼんのうklesha (polluting thoughts such as greed, hatred and delusion, which result in suffering)
    悩みごとなやみごとsomething causing worry
    悩ましげなやましげlanguishing, yearning, desiring
