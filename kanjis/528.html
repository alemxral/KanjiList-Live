及
  • Frequency:544
  • Kun: およ.ぶ, およ.び, および, およ.ぼす
  • On: キュウ
  • Meaning: reach out, exert, exercise, cause
  • Examples:

    追及ついきゅうinvestigation (e.g. into someone's guilt), questioning, pressing, hounding, pinning down; catching up, overtaking
    言及げんきゅうreference, allusion
    波及はきゅうspread, extension, influence, aftereffect, ripple
    及ぼすおよぼすto exert, to cause, to exercise
