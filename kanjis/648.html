Kanji Details:

喰
  • Frequency:0
  • Kun: く.う, く.らう
  • On: nan
  • Meaning: eat, drink, receive (a blow), (kokuji)
  • Examples:
    WordReadingMeaning
    喰うくうto eat; to live, to make a living, to survive; to bite, to sting (as insects do); to tease, to torment, to taunt, to make light of, to make fun of; to encroach on, to eat into, to consume; to defeat a superior, to threaten a position; to consume time and-or resources; to receive something (usu. an unfavourable event); to have sexual relations with a woman, esp. for the first time
    喰らうくらうto eat, to drink; to receive (e.g. a blow)
    漆喰しっくいmortar, plaster, stucco
    面喰らうめんくらうto be confused (bewildered, taken aback)
    馬喰ばくろう, はくらくcattle or horse trader, good judge of horses or cattle
    虫喰いむしくいworm-eaten; leaf warbler (any bird of genus Phylloscopus)
    一口喰うひとくちくうto have a munch, to take a bite
    飯を喰うめしをくうto devour a meal, to have a meal
    電喰でんしょくelectrolytic corrosion
    稲根喰葉虫いねねくいはむし, イネネクイハムシDonacia provostii (species of leaf beetle)
