Kanji Details:

捲
  • Frequency:0
  • Kun: ま.く, ま.くる, まく.る, めく.る, まく.れる
  • On: ケン
  • Meaning: roll, wind, coil, turn pages, roll up sleeves, strip off, be turned, be rolled up
  • Examples:
    WordReadingMeaning
    捲るまくるto turn up, to roll up (e.g. sleeves); verb suffix to indicate reckless abandon to the activity
    捲るめくるto turn over, to turn pages of a book, to tear off, to strip off
    捲れるまくれる, めくれるto be (get) turned up (inside out)
    押し捲くるおしまくるto push and push, to keep pushing to the end
    捲き上げるまきあげるto roll up, to hoist, to heave up; to take away, to rip off (e.g. money from someone); to blow up (dust)
    捲し立てるまくしたてるto talk on and on
    書き捲るかきまくるto dash off, to write freely
    吹き捲るふきまくるto blow about, to blow along, to brag
