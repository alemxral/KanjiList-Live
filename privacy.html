Privacy, Cookies, and Process of Personal Data

Effective Date: 15/08/2024

Welcome to KanjiList.org. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data. This section outlines our policies regarding the use of cookies, the process of personal data, and the measures we take to protect your information.

1. Overview

KanjiList.org does not directly collect or process personal data. However, we use third-party services like Google Analytics and Google Ads, which may collect certain information to help us analyze site traffic and improve user experience.

2. Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to analyze site performance through third-party services.

Types of Cookies Used:

  • Google Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits, duration, and other metrics. This information helps us improve our website's functionality and user experience.
  • Google Ads Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements to users who have visited our site. Google Ads may track your interaction with ads, including clicks and conversions.

Cookie Management:

You have the option to control or disable cookies through your web browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of KanjiList.org and your overall user experience.

3. Process of Personal Data

While KanjiList.org does not directly collect or process personal data, the third-party services we use, such as Google Analytics and Google Ads, may collect certain information, including but not limited to:

  • IP Address: For location-based analytics and to serve relevant ads.
  • Device Information: Such as browser type, operating system, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use and interact with our website.

Third-Party Data Processing:

The data collected by Google Analytics and Google Ads is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policies. KanjiList.org does not have access to personal data collected by these services beyond what is provided in aggregated form.

  • Google Analytics Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. While we do not directly store personal data, we rely on the security measures implemented by Google to protect any information processed through their services.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and Control: Manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of Google Analytics by using the:
    • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Request Information: Contact us to inquire about the data we collect indirectly through third-party services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy, Cookies, and Process of Personal Data section from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
